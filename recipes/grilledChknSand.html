Grilled Chicken Sandwich

Description

When its a nice day and I don't want to heat the house up, I love to fire up the grill. Originally this dish was something my mom always made for my brother and I as kids, and to this day its one of my favorites. Keep it simple stupid!

Ingredients

  • Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
  • Bread
  • Italian Dressing
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. 24-48 Hours before meal place chicken in a dish and completely coat with italian dressing. Cover and refrigerate.
  2. Prepare grill to medium high heat. Add chicken on upper most portion of grill and cook flipping every 4 mins. Once internal temperature reaches 165F pull from heat and let rest.
  3. Apply mayonnaise, tomato, lettuce and chicken to bread. Serve with you favorite side dish and enjoy!
